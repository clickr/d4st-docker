DITA Open Toolkit: General DITA Publishing and Data Processing

The DITA Open Toolkit provides general facilities for generating publishable outputs from DITA source: HTML, PDF, EPUB, etc.

The http://dita-ot.org is the primary open-source framework for generating deliverables from DITA source. It is widely used by the DITA community and integrated with most DITA-aware tools.
